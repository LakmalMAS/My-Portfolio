01 — About
Who I Am

I'm M.A.S. Lakmal (Shanaka), a 2nd-year Multimedia student at NSBM Green University, pursuing my dream of becoming a game developer and environment artist.

My focus is on building compelling, interactive worlds using Unreal Engine 5 — from level design and environment conceptualization to cinematic lighting that elevates the player's experience.

Currently developing a multiplayer co-op game set in a post-apocalyptic Sri Lanka, while also conducting academic research into barriers within Sri Lanka's game development industry.

Beyond games, I work as a 3D artist, creating visual content for advertising campaigns and branding — including real client work for hospitality brands.

My dream is to build my own game studio in Sri Lanka — creating games that tell local stories on a world stage.
